Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our diabetes prediction services

How accurate is the diabetes prediction?

Our AI model achieves 95% accuracy based on validation against medical datasets. However, this is a screening tool and should not replace professional medical diagnosis.

Is my health data secure?

Yes, we use enterprise-grade encryption and comply with HIPAA regulations to ensure your health information remains private and secure.

Can I use this instead of seeing a doctor?

No, our tool is designed for screening and early detection. Always consult with healthcare professionals for medical diagnosis and treatment decisions.

What data do I need to provide?

You'll need basic health metrics like glucose levels, BMI, blood pressure, age, and family history information for the most accurate prediction.
